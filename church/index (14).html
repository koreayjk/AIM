Who We Are

A church called to proclaim & live the Gospel

First Bible Church of Normangee is called to proclaim and live the Gospel of Christ in the way we worship, teach, and preach. We long to inspire in every person a deep love for Christ and a passion for righteousness.

We carry the Gospel first to our Jerusalem—Normangee—and from there to the ends of the earth.

  • Christ-centered, Bible-believing worship
  • A welcoming family for every generation
  • Serving our community & the world
